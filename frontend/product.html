Product Details +

The Classique Stiletto is the centrepiece of our women's collection — a refined heel that balances architectural boldness with everyday wearability.

  • Full-grain calfskin leather upper
  • Gold-tone hardware detailing
  • 9cm tapered stiletto heel
  • Leather-lined insole for all-day comfort
  • Suede non-slip sole
  • Comes in a COSMAN luxury box
Materials & Craftsmanship +

Each pair is crafted by artisan cobblers using premium full-grain leather sourced from certified tanneries. The gold hardware is hand-polished and lacquer-sealed for lasting brilliance.

  • Upper: Full-grain calfskin (Morocco, Grade A)
  • Lining: Smooth nappa leather
  • Insole: Memory foam with leather cover
  • Heel: Zinc alloy core, wrapped in leather
  • Outsole: Micro-textured rubber with suede cap
