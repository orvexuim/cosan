01 — Store Analysis

Visual DNA Extracted

Based on the physical store photos, these are the core visual signals that define COSMAN's identity — translated into digital brand language.

Architecture & Atmosphere

Matte black exterior façade with illuminated gold signage creates dramatic contrast. Floor-to-ceiling glass windows showcase curated product displays. Interior features warm white spotlights, wood-panel accent wall, and marble surfaces — a confident, high-end retail environment.

Color Signals

The store speaks in three voices: Matte Black authority, Gold prestige, and White clarity. Gold-lit shelves and warm accent lighting reinforce a premium boutique feel.

Logo Character

The CM monogram inside a perfect circle is a classic luxury emblem — geometric, bold, timeless. The full wordmark "COSMAN" uses bold serif-inspired weight with wide spacing, commanding presence and confidence.

Circular Emblem Bold Monogram Wide Letter-Spacing Geometric

Product Range & Positioning

The store carries footwear (loafers, heels, boots, sandals, sneakers) and leather bags — a complete lifestyle offer. Display style mirrors European luxury boutiques with clean shelving and curated arrangements.

Clear Space Rule

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" letter around the logo on all sides. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Minimum Size

The emblem (CM circle) must be no smaller than 32px on digital and 10mm in print. The full wordmark no smaller than 120px wide.

✓ Approved Usage

Use on #0D0D0D, #1A1A1A, white, or solid gold backgrounds only. Gold emblem on dark. Black emblem on gold/light.

✗ Prohibited

Never stretch, rotate, recolor, add drop shadows, place on busy images without overlay, or change the circular emblem shape.

✓ COSMAN Speaks Like This

  • Confident and declarative ("Step Into Luxury")
  • Elegant and minimal — no excess words
  • Timeless over trendy
  • Aspirational but accessible
  • Warm and human (not corporate cold)
  • Precise product descriptions with sensory language

✗ COSMAN Never Speaks Like This

  • Discount-heavy language ("SALE! 70% OFF!")
  • Casual slang or emoji overload
  • Generic phrases ("Best quality products")
  • Aggressive hard-sell pressure tactics
  • Overly technical jargon
  • Inconsistent capitalization or style
06 — Design Principles

Visual Philosophy

◆

Dark Luxury

Deep blacks are the canvas. Gold details are the art. Never reverse this ratio — gold should feel rare and precious.

◇

Negative Space

Let the product breathe. Premium feels spacious. Crowded layouts signal budget — empty space signals confidence.

▲

Hierarchy

One thing speaks loudest on each page. The brand name, the product, the call to action — never three things at once.

○

Gold is Sacred

Use gold purposefully — CTAs, logos, price tags, special moments. Overusing it dilutes its power and prestige.

△

Motion = Elegance

Animations should be slow and graceful. Fast = cheap. Ease-in-out at 600ms minimum for all transitions.

□

Glassmorphism

UI elements should feel like frosted glass over dark surfaces — transparent, layered, and sophisticated.
